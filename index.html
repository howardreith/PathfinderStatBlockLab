Lab Instructions

To create a new creature, input the name of your creature in the "Create New Creature" form and click the "Create New Creature" button.

The name is used for our search tool and is the only required field. The name will be added to the "Update Creature" form below automatically.

To update an existing creature, search for the creature's name in the search bar and click on its result. Its information will be added to all known fields.

To save your work, click the "Save Creature" button at the top of bottom of the form.

All fields will clear when you save your work, so make sure you've filled in everything you need!

Fields can be re-populated by once again searching for your creature in the search bar.

To delete a creature, load the creature you wish to delete using the search bar and click the "Delete Creature" button just below the "Update Creature" form.

Viewer Instructions

To view a creature, type the creature's name into the search bar in the upper-left corner of your screen.

If you do not have any creatures, you will need to create one!

To create or modify a creature, click the "Go to Lab" button.
